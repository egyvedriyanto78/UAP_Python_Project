Chainsaw man

Genre       : Action, Horror comedy, Dark Fantasy.
Tahun       : 2018
Pengarang   : Tatsuki Fujimoto
Status      : on-going
Chapter     : 107
Link        : https://mangaplus.shueisha.co.jp/titles/100037

Chainsaw Man follows the story of Denji, 
an impoverished young man who makes a contract that fuses his body with that of a dog-like devil named Pochita,
granting him the ability to transform parts of his body into chainsaws.
