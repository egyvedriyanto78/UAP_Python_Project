Spy x Family

Genre       : Action, Comedy, Fiction, Spy.
Tahun       : 2019
Pengarang   : Tatsuya Endou
Status      : on-going
Chapter     : 68
Link        : https://mangaplus.shueisha.co.jp/titles/100143

The story follows a spy who has to build a family to execute a mission, 
not realizing that the girl he adopts as his daughter is a telepath,  
and the woman he agrees to be in a marriage with is a skilled assassin.
